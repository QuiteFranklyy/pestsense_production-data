### Navigating Sensahub

<img src="/images/help/sensahub concepts/beginners guide/navigating_sensahub.png" width="934" height="589" style="display:block;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:100%;height:auto;">

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Customer Branding | Can be customised to showcase the branding of your business.|
| User Screens | Interact with the screens that you have built.|
| Screen Designer | Switch to the Screen Designer to build or modify your screens. This feature is available for designers only.|
| Flow Designer | Switch to the Flow Designer to connect Sensahub to incoming data sources. This feature is available for designers only.|
| Screen layout type | Switch between the Desktop, Phone, or Tablet layout to customise the screens for a specific device.|
| Username & Account Settings | Your username is displayed here, clicking will allow you to view your profile, change your password, or logout.|
| More | Clicking "More" lets you go to the About page to see Sensahub version information, and the Help page which contains extensive documentation on all Sensahub widgets and concepts.|
| Select Screen Tabs | Move to different screen tabs.|
| Toggle Screen Tab | Show or hide the different screen tabs.|
